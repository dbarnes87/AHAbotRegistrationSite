Commands

In general AHAbot tries to understand plain English but if you're wondering about every single thing it can do or you're having some trouble getting it to understand you we have this list of explicit commands that it will always recognize. It will only register one command per message.

General Commands

what_devices

Shows a list of devices that AHAbot can control

how_home

Shows a list of all your connected smart devices and their status

what_on

Shows a list of all your devices that are currently turned on

what_off

Shows a list of all your devices that are currently turned off

what_lights

Shows a list of your currently connected smart lights

what_switches

Shows a list of your currently connected smart plugs/switches

what_therm

Shows a list of your currently connected smart thermostats

what_media

Shows a list of your currently connected media players

Lighting Commands

light_status <room/label> (default: living room)

Shows whether a specified light is on or off

light_on <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns on a specified light

light_off <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns off a specified light

light_red <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns a specified compatible light red

light_green <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns a specified compatible light green

light_blue <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns a specified compatible light blue

light_white <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns a specified compatible light white

light_full <room/label> (default: living room)

Sets a specified light to full brightness

light_medium <room/label> (default: living room)

Sets a specified light to medium brightness

light_low <room/label> (default: living room)

Sets a specified light to low brightness

light_up <room/label> (default: living room)

Increases a specified light’s brightness by 20%

light_down <room/label> (default: living room)

Decreases a specified light’s brightness by 20%

Smart switch/plug/outlet Commands

switch_status <room/label> (default: living room)

Shows whether a specified switch/plug is on or off

switch_on <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns on a specified switch/plug

switch_off <room/label> (default: living room)

Turns off a specified switch/plug

Media Player Commands

media_status <room/label> (default: living room)

Shows whether a specified media player is on or off

media_play <room/label> (default: living room)

Unpauses playback on a specified media player

media_pause <room/label> (default: living room)

Pauses playback on a specified media player

media_stop <room/label> (default: living room)

Stops playback on a specified media player

volume_mute <room/label> (default: living room)

Mutes the audio on a specified media player

volume_up <room/label> (default: living room)

Increases the volume on a specified media player

volume_down <room/label> (default: living room)

Decreases the volume on a specified media player

Thermostat Commands

climate_status <room/label> (default: living room)

PLACEHOLDER

temperature_up <room/label> (default: living room)

Increases the temperature on the specified thermostat by 1 degree F

temperature_down <room/label> (default: living room)

Decreases the temperature on the specified thermostat by 1 degree F
